# Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## Product Title
MatchLens — AI-Powered Dating Profile Analyzer (V1)

---

## 1. Problem Statement
Dating app users struggle to get quality matches because their profiles fail to clearly communicate personality, intent, and attractiveness. Common issues include poor photo selection, generic or unclear bios, and the absence of personalized feedback. Existing dating apps do not offer actionable guidance, leaving users without a clear improvement path.

---

## 2. Target Users

### Primary Users
- College students and young professionals (18–35)
- Active dating app users
- Privacy-conscious users willing to upload profile content voluntarily

### Excluded Users (V1)
- Users under 18
- Dating coaches
- Users expecting direct dating-app integration

---

## 3. Core User Flows

### Flow 1: Profile Analysis
1. User lands on homepage
2. Uploads bio text and 1–6 photos
3. Selects dating intent (casual / serious)
4. Submits profile
5. Receives scores, insights, and suggestions

### Flow 2: Bio Improvement
1. User reviews bio analysis
2. Selects preferred bio style
3. Copies improved bio

### Flow 3: Photo Feedback
1. User reviews photo feedback
2. Reorders or removes photos based on recommendations

---

## 4. Feature List

### MVP (V1)
- Bio grammar and tone analysis
- Cliché detection
- 2–3 AI-generated bio suggestions
- Photo quality analysis (lighting, face visibility, group detection)
- Best primary photo recommendation
- Overall profile score and sub-scores
- Actionable improvement suggestions
- Session-based privacy (no login)

### Future (Out of Scope)
- Dating app integrations
- Match prediction
- A/B testing
- Premium subscriptions

---

## 5. Edge Cases
- Very short or joke-only bios
- No face visible in photos
- All group photos
- Non-English bios

---

## 6. Non-Goals
- Rating physical attractiveness
- Guaranteeing matches
- Messaging or chat suggestions

---

## 7. Success Metrics
- Analysis completion rate
- Bio copy usage rate
- Time spent on results page
- User usefulness rating
